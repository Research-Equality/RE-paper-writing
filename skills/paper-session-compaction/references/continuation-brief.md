# Continuation Brief

Use a compact structure like this:

## Current State

- latest draft path
- active objective
- target venue or review round

## Locked Decisions

- title or framing decisions
- venue assumptions
- agreed baselines, datasets, or section ordering

## Evidence and Artifacts

- result tables and figure paths
- bibliography state
- scorecards or review outputs already generated

## Open Risks

- unsupported claims
- missing experiments
- unresolved reviewer comments
- formatting or citation blockers

## Next Actions

- 1-3 concrete next steps
- exact file(s) to reopen first

## Notes on Scope

- what history was compacted
- what recent context remained unchanged
