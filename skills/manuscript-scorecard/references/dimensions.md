# Score Dimensions

Use a 1-5 scale for each dimension.

## `problem_formulation`

- 5: contribution and question are specific, important, and clearly differentiated
- 3: question exists but significance or novelty is weak
- 1: no clear research question or contribution

## `literature_review`

- 5: broad, current, synthesized, and clearly positions the paper
- 3: partial coverage or mostly descriptive listing
- 1: major gaps or weak grounding

## `methodology`

- 5: design matches the question, is reproducible, and handles major validity risks
- 3: acceptable but under-specified or with notable weaknesses
- 1: fundamentally mismatched or not reproducible

## `data_collection`

- 5: data sources, sampling, and measurement quality are credible and justified
- 3: adequate but marginal or partly undocumented
- 1: low-trust data basis or unclear provenance

## `analysis`

- 5: analytical choices are appropriate, transparent, and robust
- 3: basic analysis works but misses checks or justification
- 1: incorrect or fragile analysis

## `results`

- 5: findings are clear, well-supported, and honestly bounded
- 3: mixed clarity or weak evidence packaging
- 1: results are unclear, overstated, or incomplete

## `writing`

- 5: clear, efficient, well-structured, and easy for the target audience to follow
- 3: understandable but uneven
- 1: disorganized or hard to parse

## `citations`

- 5: relevant, balanced, complete, and well-maintained
- 3: some gaps or noisy references
- 1: weak, stale, or unreliable citation base
